# NC3ADL Toolkit

Resources developed for the North Carolina Community College Association of Distance Learning, serving all 58 NC community colleges.

## About NC3ADL

The North Carolina Community College Association of Distance Learning (NC3ADL) is a professional organization connecting distance learning leaders across the NC Community College System.

**Role:** Vice President (2024)
**Membership:** Distance learning administrators and instructional designers from all 58 NC community colleges

## Toolkit Contents

### Statewide Innovation Resources

Best practices, case studies, and implementation guides shared across the NC Community College System.

**Featured Resources:**
- Microcredentialing implementation toolkit
- AI integration guidelines for community colleges
- Quality assurance frameworks
- Distance learning technology evaluations

### Conference Materials

Resources from NC3ADL annual conferences and professional development events.

### Collaborative Projects

Multi-institution initiatives and shared resources developed through NC3ADL partnerships.
