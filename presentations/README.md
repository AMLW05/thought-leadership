## Available Presentations

### 1. Microcredentialing with A.I. (2023)
**File:** [Microcredentialing_with_AI_Westmoreland_2023.pdf](Microcredentialing_with_AI_Westmoreland_2023.pdf)

**Conference:** Credentials As You Go Conference (2023)
**Audience:** Higher education administrators and faculty nationwide
**Topic:** Strategic framework for using artificial intelligence to accelerate microcredential pathway development and assessment design. Presented the two-application model: (1) Develop the Pathway and (2) Evaluate for Mastery.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Framework adopted institution-wide at FTCC
- Scaled program from 0 to 1,014 credentials in 4 months
- Reduced credential development time by 70%

### 3. HyFlex Digital Notebook (2022)
**File:** [HyFlex_Digital_Notebook_2022.pdf](HyFlex_Digital_Notebook_2022.pdf)

**Context:** Professional development resource for faculty
**Topic:** Comprehensive guide to implementing HyFlex (Hybrid-Flexible) teaching modality. Covers course design principles, technology setup, student communication strategies, and assessment design for simultaneous in-person and remote instruction.

**Application:**
- Used for institutional HyFlex faculty training
- Template for multi-modality course design
- Best practices for flexible learning environments
